FAQ

The most-asked questions will be answered with a how-to video and added to this playlist: https://juxxi.com/viewProgram/809/how-to-use-juxxi//

What is happening to Full30?

  • Full30 will become the firearms category on Juxxi. All existing content will be migrated and will still exist. The only change for you is the location you will be uploading.

Will my existing video links on Full30 continue to work after migration?

  • Your existing videos are safe and will absolutely continue to work.

Will Full30.com go away or just point to Juxxi?

  • Full30 will point to Juxxi.com/cat/firearms

Can I migrate videos I own from other video platforms to Juxxi?

  • Yes. You can import the videos you own from most video sites. Check the Import section of the upload screen for more details.

How do I create a new category?

  • Only Juxxi Admins can create categories on Juxxi. Be sure to add tags to your video if you are trying to make it more discoverable. If you have a category recommendation, please send it to support@juxxi.com or simply use the chat feature available on Juxxi.com

How do I organize a playlist?

  • Yes. You can import the videos you own from most video sites. Check the Import section of the upload screen for more details.

How do I make my content more discoverable on Juxxi?

  • Be sure your video titles reflect the content that is in your video
  • Add a 300+ word description
  • Add tags that are related to the content in your video (i.e. topics discusses, manufacturer names, etc)
  • Share your video link with your network on social media, via email, etc.
  • Grow your subscribership (subscribers get automated notifications for each new upload)
  • Use the "notify subscribers" feature in the "My subscribers" section - be sure to use this for important videos that are extremely valuable to your subscribers. Sending too many of these emails may cause subscribers to unsubscribe from your channel.

Where do I send recommendations on site design?

  • If you have any feedback on how to improve Juxxi, please send your detailed feedback to support@juxxi.com or simply use the chat feature available here on Juxxi.com.
